Privacy Policy

YOUR PRIVACY IS FUNDAMENTAL

We don't store your messages. Period.

1. WE DON'T STORE YOUR MESSAGES

  • Immediately deleted after analysis

2. WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT

  • Message content (beyond processing)

  • Contact information
  • Historical patterns

3. MINIMAL DATA COLLECTION

  • Subscription status
  • Anonymous app metrics
  • Crash reports (no content)

4. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Export usage stats
  • Use privately

5. DATA SECURITY

  • End-to-end encryption (TLS 1.3)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • No long-term retention
  • Regular security audits
  • Privacy by design

6. THIRD PARTIES

Payment Processing:

  • Apple App Store

  • We never see payment details

Infrastructure:

  • AWS (hosting)
  • OpenAI (AI analysis)
  • Cloudflare (security)

NO advertising networks
NO data brokers
NO cross-app tracking

7. DATA RETENTION

  • Messages: Deleted immediately
  • Usage counts: Reset daily

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
