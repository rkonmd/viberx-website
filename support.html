Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

You get 3 days of premium access with 5 analyses per day. Voice messages and screenshots count toward your daily limit. After the trial, you'll need to subscribe to continue using the app. One trial per device only.

What's included in the subscription?

Monthly ($6.99): 20 daily text analyses, 10 voice messages, and 10 screenshots. Annual ($69.99): Same generous limits plus you save $14 per year. All subscriptions include GPT-4 powered analysis and priority support.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! We never store your messages. Each analysis is processed in real-time and immediately deleted. We don't sell your data, there's no human review of messages, and we use end-to-end encryption. Your privacy is fundamental to our service.

Can VibeRx replace therapy?

No. VibeRx is an educational tool only, not a replacement for professional services. It's designed to help you learn about communication patterns, but should never be used for medical advice, mental health treatment, or professional counseling. Always consult qualified professionals for serious concerns.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your App Store account settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > VibeRx and select Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Why isn't the app working?

Try these steps: 1) Check your internet connection, 2) Force quit and restart the app, 3) Check for app updates in the App Store, 4) Restart your device. If issues persist, email us at support@viberx.ai with details about the problem.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

While our GPT-4 powered analysis is sophisticated, remember that AI can make mistakes. VibeRx is designed for educational purposes to help you explore communication patterns. Always use your own judgment and verify important insights with professionals when needed.
